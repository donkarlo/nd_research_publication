\begin{frame}{Kalman filter}
    Kalamn filter is a Bayesian filter that updates the internal prediction with a new observation.
    
    There are many evidences that many mamls perform Kalman filtering for state estimation \cite{wolpert-2007-probabilistic-models-in-human-sensorimotor-control}
    
    The question is, from where the prediction is comming:
    
    In previous paper, it was from the mean of the velocities in the class.
    
    In this work, it comes from the mean of the velocities in vowel class right before the current 0th derivative cluster
    
    \maketitle
    \[
        \begin{aligned}
        (\mu^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t},\Sigma^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t})
            &=
            f_{\theta}(X_{t-L:t-1})
            &&
            \begin{array}{l}
                \mu^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t}: \text{Transformer mean prediction} \\
                \Sigma^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t}: \text{Transformer prediction covariance}
            \end{array}
            \\[8pt]
            \mu^{\mathrm{vel}}_{t}
            &=
            x_{t-1}
            +
            v\Delta t
            &&
            \begin{array}{l}
                x_{t-1}: \text{previous state} \\
                v: \text{constant velocity} \\
                \Delta t: \text{time interval}
            \end{array}
            \\[8pt]
            \Sigma^{-}_{t}
            &=
            \left[
                (\Sigma^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t})^{-1}
                +
                (\Sigma^{\mathrm{vel}}_{t})^{-1}
            \right]^{-1}
            &&
            \begin{array}{l}
                \Sigma^{-}_{t}: \text{fused prior covariance} \\
                \Sigma^{\mathrm{vel}}_{t}: \text{velocity-model covariance}
            \end{array}
            \\[8pt]
            \hat{x}^{-}_{t}
            &=
            \Sigma^{-}_{t}
            \left[
                (\Sigma^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t})^{-1}\mu^{\mathrm{tr}}_{t}
                +
                (\Sigma^{\mathrm{vel}}_{t})^{-1}\mu^{\mathrm{vel}}_{t}
            \right]
            &&
            \hat{x}^{-}_{t}: \text{fused prior estimate}
            \\[8pt]
            K_{t}
            &=
            \Sigma^{-}_{t}H^{\top}
            \left(
                H\Sigma^{-}_{t}H^{\top}
                +
                R
            \right)^{-1}
            &&
            \begin{array}{l}
                K_{t}: \text{Kalman gain} \\
                H: \text{observation matrix} \\
                R: \text{observation noise covariance}
            \end{array}
            \\[8pt]
            \hat{x}_{t}
            &=
            \hat{x}^{-}_{t}
            +
            K_{t}
            \left(
                z_{t}
                -
                H\hat{x}^{-}_{t}
            \right)
            &&
            \begin{array}{l}
                \hat{x}_{t}: \text{corrected estimate} \\
                z_{t}: \text{new observation}
            \end{array}
        \end{aligned}
    \]
