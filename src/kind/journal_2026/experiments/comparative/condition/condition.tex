        \section{Goal}
            The goal is that two robots learn what it is like to turn around a square like corrider corrider without colliding the walls by human teleportation control and then compare this experience with the experiences in which they should perform some manuvers to avoid obstacles because of the changes made in the corrider.
        \section{Involving Robots}
            During online telemetry recording, a leader-follower strategy has been practiced by the two involving UAVs in the environment. The leader is the robot to which the human operator sends control inputs. The follower follows a set of rules which are dependent on leader's positional state. As we will describe the benefits, the we have also considered human and the environment as a robots.
